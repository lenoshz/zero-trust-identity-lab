How to Enable MFA
  1. Go to your Keycloak Account Console
  2. Navigate to Account Security → Signing In
  3. Under Two-Factor Authentication, click Set up Authenticator Application
  4. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator, Authy, or any TOTP app
  5. Log out and log back in — Keycloak will prompt for TOTP
